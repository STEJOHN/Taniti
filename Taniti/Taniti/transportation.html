Transportation.

Effortless island transport awaits! Explore Taniti with ease through our buses, taxis, and rentals. Your adventure starts here.

Set sail or take flight.

Embark on a seamless journey to Taniti, where the azure waters and sandy shores await your arrival. Most visitors opt for air travel, landing at our small yet accommodating airport, which caters to both small jets and propeller planes. For those seeking an alternative adventure, a small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once a week, offering a unique opportunity to experience our island's beauty from the sea. Exciting news is on the horizon as Taniti is in the midst of expanding its airport facilities, ensuring that larger jets will soon be able to land directly on our shores, making your journey to paradise even more convenient.

Nagivate with ease.

Discover the ease of getting around Taniti with our comprehensive ground transportation options. Public buses offer convenient service to and from Taniti City, operating daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., while private buses efficiently cover the rest of the island's routes. For personalized travel, taxis are readily available in Taniti City, providing flexible transportation solutions. Looking to explore at your own pace?
