Beachfront Access

Wake up to the soothing sound of waves and step onto pristine sands just moments from your door. Our beachfront accommodations offer unparalleled access to the sun, sea, and serenity of island living.

Seclusion and Privacy

Escape the crowds and find your own slice of paradise at our secluded island retreat. Tucked away from the hustle and bustle, our private accommodations offer the perfect sanctuary for those seeking peace, tranquility, and uninterrupted relaxation.

Adventure Opportunities

Embark on a thrilling island adventure right from your doorstep. With a plethora of outdoor activities including snorkeling, diving, hiking, and island hopping, our resort is the perfect base for adrenaline junkies and nature lovers alike.

Family-Friendly Facilities

Create unforgettable family memories in a welcoming and safe environment. From supervised kids' clubs to family-friendly dining options and exciting recreational activities, our resort offers something for guests of all ages, ensuring a fun-filled and stress-free vacation for the whole family.

Dining.

Explore Taniti's 10 unique restaurants offering a variety of dishes. Choose from local fish and rice , three serve American-style classics, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine. Need groceries? Visit two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, or the 24-hour convenience store.

Fish & Rice

Discover the taste of tradition at our local fish restaurants, where each bite transports you to the heart of our coastal heritage, freshly caught and expertly prepared.

American-style Meals

Satisfy your cravings for familiar flavors and homestyle cooking at our American-style eateries, where each dish is crafted with care and served with a side of hospitality.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Delight in the artistry of Pan-Asian cuisine at our restaurants, where sushi, stir-fries, and noodle dishes come together to create an unforgettable dining experience.

Local Grocery Store

Explore a world of culinary possibilities at Taniti's grocery stores, where you'll find everything you need to cook up delicious meals at home. From our two supermarkets offering a wide range of fresh produce and pantry staples to our two smaller grocery stores with curated selections, and our convenient 24-hour convenience store, we've got you covered anytime, day or night.
